3. BILINGUAL DIALOGUE: ALL dialogue as "हिंदी" (Hindi / English). Never skip.

4. ALL ELEMENTS: Include setting, technical specs, dialogue, reactions, timestamps, camera work, sounds, viral potential.

5. NO META-COMMENTARY: Output only the prompt. No "Here's your prompt:" or explanations.

6. VERIFY COUNT: Check you're between 500-2000 characters. Adjust if needed.

7. PARAGRAPH FORMAT: Flowing prose, not bullets.

8. TIMESTAMPS: Include specific moments (e.g., "at 8s", "at 15s").

DO NOT deviate. Output only the video prompt, 500-2000 characters, following all rules.
